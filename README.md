## What is Omodo?

Omodo is a **privacy-focused AI assistant** for macOS that works entirely on your machine. It monitors your email, extracts action items, organizes your files, records meetings, and gives you a natural language chat interface to manage it all — powered by a built-in AI engine or [Ollama](https://ollama.com).

**Your data never leaves your Mac. Period.**

## Features

**Inbox to Reminders, Automatically** — Monitors Apple Mail, extracts actionable items using your local LLM, and creates reminders and calendar events in Apple's native apps. Thread-aware follow-ups can update or cancel previously created items.

**Talk to Your Data** — Ask questions in plain English. The AI chat interface has 35 tools to query, create, and manage your reminders, events, emails, notes, files, and clipboard — all with multi-step reasoning.

**Everything About a Contact, in One Place** — Track contacts and see their emails, reminders, events, notes, files, and AI-generated memory all on a single page. Builds relationship context automatically.

**Your Files, Organized Automatically** — Monitor local folders for changes. Summarizes content, extracts structured tags, generates embeddings for semantic search, and can auto-rename files based on content.

**Never Miss What Was Said** — Record meetings with on-device transcription and speaker diarization. Structured summaries saved to Apple Notes and linked to the right contacts automatically.

**Smart Clipboard** — Searchable clipboard history with auto-categorization, sensitive content detection, and app-aware smart ranking.

## Privacy

Omodo is **100% local**. All AI processing happens on your Mac — either through the built-in engine or Ollama. No cloud, no telemetry, no data collection.

## Requirements

| | |
|---|---|
| **Mac** | Apple Silicon (M1 or later) |
| **macOS** | 14 Sonoma or later |
| **LLM** | Built-in (no setup needed), or [Ollama](https://ollama.com) for more model choices |

## Install

1. Download the latest `.dmg` from the [Releases](https://github.com/poyi/Omodo-releases/releases/latest) page
2. Open the `.dmg` and drag Omodo to Applications
3. Right-click the app and select **Open** (required for unsigned builds)
4. Follow the onboarding wizard to grant permissions and configure your LLM

> **Note:** Omodo is currently unsigned and unnotarized. You'll need to right-click → Open on first launch to bypass Gatekeeper.
